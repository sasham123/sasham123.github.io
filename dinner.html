Sasha's Breaded Chicken

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breast slices
  • 2 eggs
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic salt
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon

Preparation

  1. Pound chicken breast into very thin slices
  2. Season with garlic salt, salt, and pepper
  3. Whisk eggs in a bowl
  4. Place panko breadcrumbs in another bowl
  5. Cover pan with olive oil and place on medium heat
  6. Wait a couple minutes for oil to heat up
  7. Dip chicken slices into eggs and then directly into panko crumbs
  8. Place breaded chicken slices onto pan for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown
  9. Sprinkle lemon juice over chicken and enjoy! I usually eat this with a serving of pasta and vodka sauce.

Sasha's Burrito Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground chicken
  • 1/2 packet of taco seasoning
  • 1 pack of Trader Joe's instant brown rice
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Handful of cherry tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/2 mini cucumber, finely chopped
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, finely chopped
  • Guacamole
  • Sour cream
  • Garlic salt
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. Drizzle pan with avocado oil and place on medium heat
  2. Season ground chicken with garlic salt, salt, and pepper
  3. Cook on pan over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally
  4. Once chicken is fully cooked, mix in taco seasoning with some water and stir
  5. Heat up instant brown rice in microwave
  6. Chop up tomatoes, cucumber, and lettuce
  7. Assemble bowl in following order: rice, seasoned chicken, handful of shredded mozzarella cheese, lettuce, cucumber, and tomatoes
  8. Season with salt and pepper and top off with a spoonful of guacamole and sour cream
  9. Enjoy this burrito bowl with tortilla chips!
