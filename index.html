Ever since I was little, cooking has always been one of my favorite activities. I used to love helping my parents cook dinner every night, and now love making my favorite foods for my friends and family.

Finding the time to cook proper meals in college can be a little tough, but over the years I have come up with various recipes that are easy to make, affordable, and delicious.

Making a tasty, nutritious breakfast is an important way for me to start the day off right. Some of my favorite dishes to make include omelettes, avocado toast, and breakfast burritos.

I always find myself getting hungry in between my classes, so I love to make a quick bite to eat when I have a break. Some of my favorite lunch meals include salads, sandwiches, and quesadillas.

Dinner is probably my favorite meal of the day. When I'm at school, I love cooking for my roommates and friends that come over. My favorite dishes to make include spicy vodka pasta, at-home burrito bowls, and pizzas.

Read my recipes and follow along to cook some amazing college meals!
